Minority languages of Southeast and East Asia

In this week I introduce fieldwork on documenting, understanding, learning from and preserving the Abui Language. The principal researcher is František Kratchovíl, but many, many people are involved.

Readings and Resources

  • Slides: Doing things together: multimodal & multidisciplinary fieldwork experiences from eastern Indonesia (Kratchovíl et al., 2021)
  • Abui Hanoting: A collection of videos about the Abui tribe in Alor Island, Indonesia. This channel consists of Abui interviews, stories, and general recordings of people, and environment.
  • Abui, Takpala, Alor Video by Gary Bevan (2013)
  • Open Multilingual Wordnet
