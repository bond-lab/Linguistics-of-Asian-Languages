Presentation of seminar work topics and its structure

This week you will briefly present the topics of your seminar papers.

Presentation

  • Presentations can be done in a group of max. 3 students or individually.
  • The choice of topic is free, but the topic should be approved before the lesson in week 12.
  • There will be 10 minutes, 7 for presentation, 3 for QA
  • Upload your paper one day before presentation.
  • Formatting
    • Please make sure your names are on the slides
    • Please add page numbers to each slide (except optionally the first one)

Paper

  • Seminar papers can be written in a group of max. 3 students (e.g. as in presentations) or individually.
  • Normally it should be the same as your presentation, although if you decide you reaaly want to do something else, email me.
  • The individual seminar paper should be at least 5 pages long.
  • Group seminar work should be proportionally more extensive, e.g. 5-6 pages for one student; 7-8 pages for two, 9-10 pages for 3, not including references.
  • Seminar work can be overview (research), research, creative, etc.
  • The submission deadline is end of January (end of day, anywhere in the world).
  • Submit pdf via moodle
  • Formatting
    • The paper should be 5-10 A4 pages, depending on how many students, and include the title, date and your name(s) on the first page, you do not need a separate title page.
    • The paper should be single spaced.
    • If you want to make it even more beautiful, as I am sure you do, take a look at my (Computational) Linguistic Style Guidelines: a guide for the flummoxed.
